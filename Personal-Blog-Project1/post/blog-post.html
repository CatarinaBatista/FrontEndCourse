Introduction

Bacalhau com natas (or in english, cod cod with creams) is a typical dish in Portugal! It is one of the most appreciated ways to serve cod and it is one of my favorites foods.

Difficulty: medium

Time of preparation: 80 min

People: 4

Ingredients

  • 500 g potato
  • 125 ml oil
  • 400 g cod
  • 40 g butter
  • 40 g flour
  • 250 ml milk
  • 250 ml cream
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of nutmeg
  • Pepper
  • Lemon drops
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 onion
  • 100 g grated mozzarella cheese

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 220º C.
  2. Dice the potatoes and fry them in olive oil.
  3. Put the cod in a pan and cover it with water. Bring it to a boil over high heat, when it boils turn down the heat and cook 5 minutes.
  4. Remove cod from water and set aside 200 ml of water. Let the cod cool, remove the skin, bones and shred it.
  5. Bring a pan to a boil, then butter until melted. Add flour and stir well. Gradually, without stirring, add the water to cook the reserved cod, milk and cream. When it begins to boil, season with salt, pepper, nutmeg and a few drops of lemon. Wrap all ingredients well and remove from heat. Reserve.
  6. In another pan, pour 100 ml of olive oil and cook the onions, garlic and bay leaf. Add the cod, stir and sauté a little.
  7. Add the potatoes and wrap well. Then add 2/3 of the béchamel sauce, stir well remove from heat and set aside.
  8. Brush an oven tray with the remaining olive oil and spread the cod preparation well. Put the remaining bechamel sauce and sprinkle with the grated cheese.
  9. Bake cod with cream with cream in the oven to brown for 15 to 20 minutes.
  10. Enjoy it! :)
